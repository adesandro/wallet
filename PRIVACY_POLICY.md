# Privacy Policy for Modulr Wallet

**Last updated: January 14, 2026**

## Overview

Modulr Wallet is a browser extension that allows you to manage cryptocurrency accounts on the Modulr blockchain. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection and Storage

### What We Store Locally

Modulr Wallet stores all user data **locally in your browser** using Chrome's encrypted storage API:

- **Wallet data**: Account names, public keys, and encrypted private keys
- **Transaction history**: Records of transactions you've sent
- **Settings**: Your configured node URL

### What We Do NOT Collect

We do **NOT**:
- Collect personal information
- Send your data to external servers (except transaction requests to your configured blockchain node)
- Track usage analytics or behavior
- Use cookies or tracking technologies
- Share any data with third parties

## Security

- Your wallet password is used to encrypt all sensitive data using AES-GCM encryption
- Private keys are encrypted and never stored in plain text
- Your password and private keys **never leave your device**
- We cannot recover your password or keys if lost

## Network Requests

The extension makes network requests only to:
- **Your configured Modulr node** â€” to fetch account balances and submit transactions

No other external connections are made.

## Data Retention

All data is stored locally in your browser. You can delete all wallet data at any time using the "Reset wallet" function in Settings.

## Your Rights

You have full control over your data:
- Export your accounts as JSON at any time
- Delete all data by resetting the wallet
- Uninstall the extension to remove all stored data

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
